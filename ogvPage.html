The Process

This is the process that you will go trough to go in an opportunity abroad with.

Sign up on AIESEC.org

Create your account, you will be contacted by a manager from AIESEC in 48h.

Apply

Fill out your profile and apply for opportunities, your manager will be helping with providing opportunities.

Interview

You will be contacted for an interview by the opportunity manager from the host entity for a professional interview with the school or the instistution

Approved

Once the interview is completed successfully, pay the AIESEC fees and sign the contracts to get approved

Preparation

AIESEC will now support in visa, departure and experience abroad
